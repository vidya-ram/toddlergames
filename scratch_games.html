Games developed in Scratch

Scratch is s a programming language, designed and maintained by the Lifelong Kindergarten group at the MIT Media Lab. Scratch is a great way to introduce programming to children. With Scratch they can create animations, games. They learn important computational concepts (iteration, conditionals, variables, functions) and helps develop other skills like maths and logic.
